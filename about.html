Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) is a prominent educational organization in India, established by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Founded in 1963, KVS manages a vast network of Kendriya Vidyalayas (Central Schools) across the country and abroad. These schools aim to provide quality education to the children of government employees, ensuring a uniform curriculum and fostering national integration. KVS is committed to promoting academic excellence, holistic development.

The Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) libraries stand as enriching hubs of knowledge, playing a pivotal role in the holistic development of students. These libraries, integral to each Kendriya Vidyalaya (Central School), serve as dynamic spaces that extend beyond textbooks. With an extensive collection of books, journals, and multimedia resources, KVS libraries foster a love for reading and independent research. KVS libraries are designed to cater to the diverse interests and academic needs of students.
